# Future Ideas

These ideas are intentionally not part of the current source-curation and weekly backfill round.

## Later, Not Now

- Personal interest profiles: requires accounts, long-term behavior data, preference storage, and privacy work.
- Personalized morning briefings: depends on reading history, likes, saves, comments, and stable user identity.
- Podcast output: needs script generation, TTS, audio storage, covers, playback, and distribution.
- TTS daily reports: higher cost and a larger experience surface than the current text report.
- Immersive translation: AIWatch is Chinese-summary-first, not a full bilingual reading layer.
- Heavy recommendation algorithms: current data volume and feedback loops are not mature enough.
- Full reader ecosystem: subscription import/export, social following, and complex inbox workflows can wait.

## Research Directions

- News-style current hotspot UX: same-event clustering, multi-source evidence, and fast scanning.
- Subscription management UX: clearer source grouping, health state, and review flows.
- Optional WeChat RSS adapter: only if self-hosted or legally stable, and always marked with high dependency risk.
- Docker/deploy improvements: safer one-command production update, backup checks, and health probes.
- Push delivery: only after the core feed, reports, and source quality are stable.
